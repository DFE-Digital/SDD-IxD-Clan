Interaction Design Clan Charter

Our pledge to each other

Defined by the Clan, to state our values, our culture and how we will work together.

  • Respect team members
  • Have a shared purpose and meaning
  • Listen
  • Have a managable workloads to avoid burn out
  • Share knowledge so we can all learn
  • Ask questions before making assumptions
  • Support each other
  • Be aware that we have different skillsets; it's okay to not know everything
  • Be kind
