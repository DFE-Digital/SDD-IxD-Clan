Interaction Design Clan Charter

Our pledge to each other

Defined by the Clan, to state our values, our culture and how we will work together. The team principles are connected, like a jigsaw, with each one being equally important to the full picture of us as a Clan and our culture

  • Respect team members
  • Have a shared purpose and meaning
  • Listen
  • Have a managable workloads to avoid burn out
  • Share knowledge so we can all learn
  • Ask questions before making assumptions
  • Support each other
  • It's ok to not know everything - we have different skillsets
  • Be kind
